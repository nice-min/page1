Install and launch apps in Registry app.

This is kitchen sink sample. It demonstrates all UI components and themes and dashboard. You can launch demo in webkit based browser (Chrome, Safari, Mobile Safari, Android Browser)

Launch in new tab
Tip1:   Press long on the bottom area of left iPhone screen to make systemdock visible.
Tip2:   Scan the QR code with your smartphone!
Tip3:   Navigate to following URL in your smartphone browser. http://www.econovation.co.kr/mobello/demo/launcher.html

Setting

You can change current theme or font with Setting app.

Registry

You can find additional apps to install from the Registry app. You can search and install apps onto the dashboard(homescreen).
